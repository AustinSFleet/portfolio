Projects

Battle Math is a project that I dearly love. Battle math is a react app that aims to incorporate learning and video games, targeting elementary aged students. The goal is simple but I highly encourage you to create a profile and explore it yourself! It is deployed through heroku and was built in 7 days. I really wanted to foster an environment of exploration, and as you level up, you'll gain more abilities to defeat the tougher enemies. Future plans include to scale to a larger world, react really makes it simple to scale and add more monsters and increase the difficulty of math questions available.




Plot Holes was a two day project made strictly with HTML,CSS, and Javascript. It's deployed through github and was a small group project working through using external APIs. Plot Holes is a simple trivia game that uses OMDB for plots and depending on whether you win or lose, you'll receive a celebratory or condemning gif, respectively! The gifs come courtesy of the Giphy API, and was a really fun quick app to develop.
